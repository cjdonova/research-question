Evaluate Your Question

Quality checklist

☐ Does my question allow for multiple possible answers? +
Your question shouldn't have just one obvious answer. Good research questions are open-ended.
☐ Is it testable with available research methods? +
You should be able to imagine how researchers could study this using surveys, data analysis, or observations.
☐ Is the question clear and precise? +
Someone else should understand exactly what you're asking. Avoid vague terms.
